## FAQ
1. **How much does this cost?**
   SpendLens is 100% free. We build it to help startups optimize their spend and to introduce them to Credex’s discounted infrastructure credits.
2. **Do I need to connect my bank or tool accounts?**
   No. SpendLens is a privacy-first tool. You simply input your tiers and seats; we never ask for API keys or login credentials to your tools.
3. **How accurate is the pricing data?**
   Our pricing engine is updated weekly against official vendor pages. Every recommendation is backed by defensible financial reasoning.
4. **Is my data public?**
   Only if you choose to share it. When you generate a shareable link, we strip all identifying PII (like your email or company name) to keep the report safe for internal review.
5. **How does Credex make money?**
   We help high-spend startups purchase AI infrastructure credits (Cursor, Claude, etc.) at significant discounts. SpendLens helps identify when you’re ready for those credits.
6. **Can I export the report?**
   Yes. After the audit, you can download a professional PDF report to share with your finance team or investors.
